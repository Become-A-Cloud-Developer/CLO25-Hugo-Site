# Course Book

The CLO25 Course Book is the textbook companion to the hands-on exercises. Read it end-to-end, or jump to the Part that matches the week you're in.

The book has ten Parts. Parts I and II cover the foundations every cloud developer needs. Parts III and IV develop the application-development half of the BCD course. Parts V through X cover the advanced topics — identity, services, containers, delivery, observability, and collaboration — that make up the ACD course.

## How to read this book

Each Part is independent enough that you can pick it up cold, but reading in order pays off — terminology and worked examples build on each other. Every chapter pairs with at least one practical exercise; the chapter introduces the concept and the exercise gives you the muscle memory.

Each chapter ends with a **Summary** section that recaps its load-bearing claims in a few sentences. If you're scanning, read the summary first.
