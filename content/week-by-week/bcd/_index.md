# BCD Weekly Schedule

Basic Cloud Development — 26 January to 5 April 2026 (calendar weeks 5–14). Three thematic blocks:

1. **Infrastructure and web development (Weeks 1–3)** — virtual servers, IaC, web development
2. **Networks, architecture, and storage (Weeks 4–6)** — virtual networks, three-tier architecture, storage
3. **DevOps and operations (Weeks 7–9)** — CI/CD, secret management, monitoring

Week 10 is reserved for review and wrap-up.
