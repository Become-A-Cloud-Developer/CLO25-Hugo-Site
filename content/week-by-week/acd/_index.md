# ACD Weekly Schedule

Advanced Cloud Development — 6 April to 29 May 2026 (calendar weeks 15–22). Two thematic blocks:

1. **Development methodology, containerization, and deployment (Weeks 1–4)** — agile workflow, Docker, authentication, CI/CD
2. **Observability, API design, and cloud services (Weeks 5–7)** — logging, REST APIs, file handling, health checks

Week 8 is reserved for review, summary, and the closing demo.

Each week consists of two on-campus full days (Wed–Thu) plus a Friday afternoon online session where students demo their ongoing application development.
