# BCD Course

Grundläggande Molnapplikationer (50p) - Basic Cloud Development for Cloud Developers.

## Course Structure

The course runs for 10 weeks and is organized into three thematic blocks:

| Block | Weeks | Theme |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 1 | 1–3 | Infrastructure and Web Development |
| 2 | 4–6 | Network, Architecture, and Storage |
| 3 | 7–9 | DevOps and Operations |
| — | 10 | Wrap-up |

## Assessment

Three individual assignments, one per block:

| Assignment | Covers |
|------------|--------|
| Assignment 1 | Weeks 1–3 |
| Assignment 2 | Weeks 4–6 |
| Assignment 3 | Weeks 7–9 |

Assignments are submitted as PDF on Google Classroom.

## Schedule

Each week follows a consistent pattern:

- **Monday** (Campus, full day) — Theory and introduction
- **Thursday** (Campus, full day) — Hands-on lab
- **Friday** (Online, half day) — Demo

See the [weekly schedule](/week-by-week/bcd/) for detailed content per week.
